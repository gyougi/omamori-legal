English

Data collected: none.

The App requires no account and performs no server transmission, user tracking, or sharing with third parties.

  • Location: not used.
  • Bluetooth: used only to detect the connection/disconnection between your iPhone and Apple Watch. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Notifications: used to show the disconnect alarm on your device.
  • Purchases: the one-time purchase is handled by Apple (App Store / StoreKit); the App never receives your payment or personal information.

No advertising or analytics SDKs are used. Contact: gyou.gi@glspace.co.jp
